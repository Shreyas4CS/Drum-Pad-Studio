How to use: Pick a Connection Type above — USB Serial (plug ESP32 into this device via cable, then click Connect and choose the COM port) or Bluetooth (ESP32 broadcasts wirelessly as "CognoSpace-DrumPad" — click Connect and pick it from the list; great for playing sound through your tablet's speaker). Each pad shows live distance. Drag the slider to set trigger threshold. When hand enters range, the pad fires its sound. Switch modes to change the sound kit. Admin: login with admin / admin123 to test sounds without the sensor.
